FDA-Approved PPG Heart Rate

Our photoplethysmography sensor has FDA clearance for medical-grade heart rate monitoring. Multi-wavelength LED technology provides accuracy within ±1 BPM across all skin tones and activity levels.

Clinical SpO2 Monitoring

Continuous blood oxygen saturation tracking using dual-wavelength infrared technology. Hospital-grade precision for sleep apnea detection and altitude training optimization.

6-axis accelerometer and gyroscope combination eliminates motion artifacts from heart rate readings. Advanced algorithms distinguish between arm movement and cardiovascular signals with 99.7% accuracy.
