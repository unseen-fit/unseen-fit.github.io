Built for Life

UNSEEN represents a fundamental shift in health tracking technology. Unlike traditional wearables that compete for wrist space, UNSEEN operates invisibly on your upper arm, delivering medical-grade insights without disrupting your life.

Advanced Sensor Technology: Features FDA-approved PPG sensors for heart rate, HRV, and SpO2 monitoring. Multi-wavelength LED technology provides accuracy within ±1 BPM across all skin tones and activity levels.

Ultra-Lightweight Design: At just 8.6 grams, UNSEEN is barely noticeable. The ABS+PC blend enclosure provides durability while maintaining comfort for continuous wear. IP68 water resistance (50m) means it handles your active lifestyle.

Wear-and-Forget Experience: With up to 10-day battery life and magnetic charging, UNSEEN is designed for continuous wear without the hassle of daily charging. The 6-axis motion sensors provide precise activity tracking.

Designed for Health

UNSEEN's intelligent algorithms process data from advanced sensors to provide comprehensive health insights that help you understand your body better than ever before.

Activity Index: Our revolutionary health metric combines cardiovascular fitness, recovery patterns, sleep quality, stress levels, and metabolic health into a single, meaningful score that helps identify health trends before they become problems.

Comprehensive Health Metrics: Track calories burned, steps taken, sleep stages, activity levels, recovery status, heart rate variability (HRV), blood oxygen levels (SpO2), and vital signs. Get insights into your cardiovascular health, stress levels, and overall wellness.

Privacy First: UNSEEN processes all health data locally on your device, not in the cloud. Your health information is never monetized, and you maintain complete ownership and control of your data with end-to-end encryption.
