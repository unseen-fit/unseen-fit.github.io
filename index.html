Frequently Asked Questions

Why upper arm placement instead of wrist?

Upper arm placement frees your wrist for watches and jewelry, provides superior accuracy for heart rate monitoring, and is completely hidden under clothing for a seamless experience.

What makes UNSEEN different from other health trackers?

UNSEEN offers no subscriptions, verified measurement accuracy, privacy-first design with on-device processing, and a wear-and-forget experience with 10-day battery life and magnetic charging.

What sensors does UNSEEN include?

UNSEEN features heart rate, motion, HRV (heart rate variability), and SpO2 (blood oxygen) sensors for comprehensive health monitoring with medical-grade accuracy.
