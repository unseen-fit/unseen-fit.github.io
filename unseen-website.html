Frequently asked questions

Why wear it on the bicep?

The bicep location serves multiple purposes: it's required for healthcare workers who must maintain bare-below-elbow protocols, protects the device from damage in industrial settings, and stays completely out of the way during daily activities.

How accurate is it compared to wrist trackers?

UNSEEN uses medical-grade PPG sensors that perform equally well on the bicep. Our dual 3D sensor system and advanced algorithms ensure accuracy comparable to or better than leading wrist-worn devices.

Is it really no subscription?

Yes, absolutely. You pay $149 once and get everything forever. All features, all app updates, all your data. We believe your health data shouldn't be held hostage by monthly fees.

How does it work without a screen?

UNSEEN syncs automatically with your phone via Bluetooth. Check your stats when you want to, not when a screen demands your attention. A subtle vibration alerts you for sedentary reminders.

What's the Vitality Score?

Based on groundbreaking NTNU research with over 1 million participants, your Vitality Score measures physical fitness relative to biological age. A score of 100 is associated with 25% lower mortality risk.

Is it comfortable for 24/7 wear?

At just 8.6g with a soft nylon strap, UNSEEN is incredibly lightweight and comfortable. The adjustable velcro closure fits all arm sizes, and the low-profile design stays hidden under clothing.
